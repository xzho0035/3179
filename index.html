What is the current distribution of forest cover in Australia?

This map indicates the forest cover ratio in Australia. The forest cover ratio is the land amount that is covered by forest.

Based on the map shown right-hand side, we can find that Tasmania has the highest forest cover ratio, 0.6. And South Australia has the lowest forest cover ratio.


What is the changes in forest area in Australia?

This bar chart is used to show the changes of forest area in the three states with the highest forest areas from 2002 to 2020.

Based on the information shown above, we can find that between 2002 and 2014, the total forest area in Western Australia, New South Wales and Victoria has INCREASWS. However, from 2014 to 2020, the forest area in these states declined.


How much the forest area in each state?

This bar chart is used to show the forest area for each state in 2021.

Based on the information shown above, we can find that New South Wales, Victoria and Western Australia are still the three states with the MOST forested areas in 2021.

Tree species in the Australian forests

This pie chart shows the different tree species in Australian forests.

Based on the information shown on the left, we can see that Eucalypt is the most abundant tree species in Australian forests, which reaching 3/4 of the total.
